Promote this cycle as a confirmatory reproduction and boundary-setting artifact.

The repo can now say, more cleanly:

- the current 50-case v3 battery reproduces the strong Gemini targeted-axis
  measurement result on the exact saved file
- the same exact battery also reproduces the weak raw `good/bad` result

  not a minimalist bare-word interface

   stale, accidental, or confounded by hidden length drift.

   because those are the main remaining weak spots in the routed-axis pass.
