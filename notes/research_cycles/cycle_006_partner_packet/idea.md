# Cycle 006 Idea

Date: June 27, 2026

Cycle: `cycle_006_partner_packet`

## Question

Can the repo present its current evidence in a way that an investor, partner,
or skeptical collaborator could inspect quickly without being misled?

## Why This Matters

The project now has enough results that a bad summary would be dangerous:

- the repo has real objective reranking evidence;
- it has a strong capability gap;
- it has an honest negative result on raw `good/bad`;
- it has a promising but incomplete process result.

If those are scattered across many folders and old drafts, outside readers will
either miss the strongest evidence or over-read the weakest evidence.

## Falsification

Treat the packet as inadequate if:

- it hides failures or negative results;
- it implies the training claim already passed when the gate failed;
- it cites outdated numbers instead of current saved outputs;
- or it cannot be regenerated from the current report artifacts.
